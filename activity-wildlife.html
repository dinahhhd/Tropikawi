Wildlife Activities

Langkawi wildlife park

From feeding colorful parrots to watching raccoons play, Langkawi Wildlife Park offers interactive experiences perfect for families and animal lovers

Opening hours: 8:30 AM – 5:00 PM daily. Popular activities offered here are feeding parrots, rabbits, ostriches, and fish, walk-in aviary with tropical birds and educational talk shows and animal interactions

Know more

Underwater world Langkawi

Walk through a 15-meter-long underwater tunnel where sharks, giant stingrays, and graceful sea turtles swim right above your head

Opening Hours: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM daily. Popular activities offered here are penguin feeding show, observe marine life like sharks, stingrays, turtles, jellyfish, penguins and learn about marine ecosystems in themed zones

Know more

Crocodile adventureland

Get up close with over 1,000 crocodiles at Crocodile Adventureland, Langkawi’s thrilling wildlife park dedicated entirely to these ancient reptiles

Opening Hours: 9:30 AM – 6:00 PM daily. Activities that can be done here are watching crocodile feeding show, see giant crocodile named “Bujang Lang”, hold and take pictures with baby crocodiles and walk across bridges and view crocs from above

Know more

Langkawi nature park

Escape into the peaceful greenery of Langkawi Nature Park, where wildlife, rainforest, and relaxation come together in one scenic eco-park

Opening Hours: 12:00 PM – 7:00 PM daily. Interesting activities here are explore mangrove boardwalks, spot birds and small animals in their enclosures and enjoy nature walks along forest trails

Know more

Kilim geoforest park

Get up close with Langkawi’s fascinating wildlife as you explore Kilim Geoforest Park’s mangroves, home to monkeys, snakes, mudskippers, and rare birds

Visit to Bat Cave (Gua Kelawar) with hundreds of fruit bats, eagle watching activity, floating fish farm visit and mangrove wildlife spotting

Know more

Dev's Adventure Tours Sdn. Bhd.

Explore Langkawi’s rich biodiversity with Dev’s Adventure Tours, where each trip is designed to be educational, interactive, and respectful of the natural environment

Interesting wildlife activities here are bird watching, spotting otters and eagles on a mangrove kayaking tour, and encountering monkeys and exotic insects during rainforest trekking

Know more

JungleWalla -Natural History Tours

Discover Langkawi’s wild side with JungleWalla, where expert guides take you on unforgettable rainforest walks, mangrove cruises, and bird-watching adventures. All while uncovering the island’s hidden natural wonders
