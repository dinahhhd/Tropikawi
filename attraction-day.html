Daytime Attractions


Dataran Helang

Located in Kuah Town, this stunning waterfront plaza features a magnificent 12-meter-tall sculpture of a reddish-brown eagle poised for flight-symbolizing the island's name and spirit. Surrounded by serene waters, beautifully landscaped terraces, and picturesque bridges, Dataran Helang is perfect for a relaxing stroll, memorable photos, or simply soaking in the scenic views of the harbor. Whether you're visiting at sunrise or under the evening lights, it's a must-see destination that captures the essence of Langkawi.



Skywalk

Experience breathtaking views like never before at the Langkawi Skywalk, one of the island's most thrilling attractions. Suspended high above Gunung Mat Cincang, this curved pedestrian bridge offers panoramic vistas of lush rainforests, dramatic mountain peaks, and the shimmering Andaman Sea. Accessible via the Langkawi Cable Car, the Skywalk combines adventure with awe-inspiring scenery, making it a must-visit for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike. With its unique design and glass-bottom sections for a heart-pounding perspective, the Langkawi Skywalk promises an unforgettable journey above the clouds.



Skybridge

Step into the sky at the Langkawi Sky Bridge, a world-renowned architectural marvel suspended 660 meters above sea level. Stretching 125 meters across the peak of Gunung Mat Cincang, this curved suspension bridge offers jaw-dropping views of Langkawi's emerald rainforests, rugged mountains, and the glistening sea beyond. Accessible via an exhilarating cable car ride, the Sky Bridge features viewing platforms and glass panels for a thrilling, unobstructed look beneath your feet. Whether you're a photographer, adventurer, or nature enthusiast, the Langkawi Sky Bridge delivers an unforgettable experience at the top of the island.



Cable Car

Soar above the treetops with the Langkawi Cable Car, also known as the SkyCab-an unmissable experience that takes you on a breathtaking journey to the peak of Gunung Mat Cincang, Langkawi's second-highest mountain. The 15-minute ride offers panoramic views of lush rainforest, cascading waterfalls, and distant islets scattered across the Andaman Sea. Along the way, make a stop at the scenic viewing platforms and enjoy cool mountain breezes and unforgettable photo opportunities. With glass-bottom gondolas and 360-degree vistas, the Langkawi Cable Car is the perfect blend of adventure and natural beauty, making it a must-do for every visitor.
