About Us


Welcome to Tropikawi! – Your Gateway to Langkawi's Natural Wonders

Tropikawi is a trusted travel agency based in the heart of Langkawi, Malaysia’s most loved tropical island. Since our founding in 2018, we have been committed to helping travelers from all over the world explore the beauty, culture, and charm of this island paradise. Whether you're visiting for the first time or returning for another adventure, our goal is to make your experience unforgettable.

At Tropikawi, we believe that Langkawi is more than just a destination, it’s a place full of hidden gems waiting to be discovered. From stunning white sand beaches and clear turquoise waters to lush green rainforests and majestic waterfalls, the island has something for everyone. That’s why we offer a wide variety of travel packages and experiences that are carefully designed to suit different travel styles, budgets, and interests.

Our team is made up of passionate locals who know Langkawi inside and out. We take pride in sharing the stories, traditions, and natural beauty of our island with every visitor. Whether you're looking for a peaceful escape, a fun filled family holiday, or an adventurous trip filled with activities, we’re here to guide you every step of the way.

Let Tropikawi show you the best that Langkawi has to offer from the popular attractions to the hidden treasures only the locals know about.
